About

Coffee is a beloved beverage made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of the Coffea plant. It has a rich history that dates back centuries, originating in Ethiopia before spreading to the Arabian Peninsula and beyond.

There are two main species of coffee beans: Arabica, known for its smooth, complex flavors, and Robusta, which is stronger and often more bitter. Coffee is typically brewed using various methods, including drip, espresso, French press, and pour-over, each producing distinct flavors and strengths.
